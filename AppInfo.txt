Following features are available in this Attendance system app

1. Creating new Batch and student.
2. Creating student for each batch
   Can choose 2 days/week or 3 days/week. 
   2 days represent - Tuesday & Thursday
   3 days represent - Tuesday, Thursday, Friday
3. Marking attendance for each student
4. View attendance
5. Calculate fees based on their selected schedule and 
   no of days present
